Radoinsko Lake

Radoinjsko Jezero is located along the Uvac Canyon, on the Zlatar mountain range, at 805 meters above sea level. The lake has been declared a first category natural good in Serbia as part of the Uvac Special Nature Reserve.

Lazar's Canyon

Lazar's Canyon is located at about 10 kilometers from Bor. This is the deepest and longest canyon in eastern Serbia. Because of its steep rocky cliffs, the canyon has not yet been fully examined. The canyon is famous for its numerous caves and pits.
